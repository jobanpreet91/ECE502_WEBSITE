Supporting Children’s Language Development at Home

Welcome to this website created to support families in understanding children’s language development. Early language skills are essential for children’s communication, learning, wellbeing, and social relationships.

This website provides clear and practical information about how children develop speaking and listening skills from birth to five years. It also explains how families can support language development through everyday interactions at home.

The information shared values cultural and linguistic diversity and supports families from all backgrounds to feel confident in supporting their child’s communication development.
